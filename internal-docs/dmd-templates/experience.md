# Experience Template

## Overview
The `experience` template is used for documenting professional work experience.

## Frontmatter

The following fields in the frontmatter require manual input:

*   `title`: `<title>` - The title of the role or position.
*   `date`: `<creation_date>` - The creation date.
*   `card.columnid`: `<column>` - The column ID for the board view.
*   `card.datebox`: `<Nov_2004_â€“_Mar_2014>` - The duration of the employment.
*   `card.position`: `<position>` - The position of the card.
*   `card.title`: `<place_or_company>` - The name of the company or organization.

## Body Content

The following sections after `---` require manual input:

*   `[summary:string]`: A brief summary of the role.
*   `[duration:string]`: The duration string.
*   `[place_url:string]`: URL of the company/organization.
*   `[place:string]`: Name of the company/organization.
*   `[content:md]`: Detailed content describing responsibilities and achievements (Markdown allowed).
*   `[projects:md]`: Specific projects worked on during this role (Markdown allowed).
