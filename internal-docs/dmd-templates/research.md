# Research Template

## Overview
The `research` template is used for documenting research ideas, hypotheses, and knowledge.

## Frontmatter

The following fields in the frontmatter require manual input:

*   `title`: `<title>` - The title of the research topic.
*   `date`: `<creation_date>` - The creation date.
*   `card.columnid`: `<column>` - The column ID for the board view.
*   `card.content`: `<short_description>` - A short description for the card.
*   `card.position`: `<position>` - The position of the card.

## Body Content

The following sections after `---` require manual input:

*   `[summary:string]`: A brief summary of the research.
*   `[idea:md]`: The core idea or hypothesis (Markdown allowed).
*   `[speculations:md]`: Speculations and potential future directions (Markdown allowed).
*   `[knowledge:md]`: Established knowledge or findings (Markdown allowed).
