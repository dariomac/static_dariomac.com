Interview with Agencia EFE about Soft Skills

Nov 2022 - 1hr

Agencia EFE

An interview with Agencia EFE about Soft Skills and how they are important for developers, delving into the essential interpersonal and communication abilities that complement technical expertise.

EFE is the leading news agency in Spanish, with 82 years of experience and a worldwide network of journalists. More than 3,000 professionals of 60 nationalities work from more than 110 countries and with five editing desks in Madrid, Bogota, Cairo, Rio de Janeiro and Bangkok.

The interview was conducted by Luis Amaya, a Social Communication professional with more than seven years of experience in the areas of Opinion and New Media at El País newspaper in Cali, the Spanish Agency EFE in Bogota, and two and a half years as Deputy International Editor of El Tiempo newspaper and in the digital area of CNN en Español.
